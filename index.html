FAQ

Questions We
Hear Often

Will dental treatment be painful?
+
We use the latest local anesthesia techniques and gentle approaches to ensure you feel minimal to no discomfort. Most patients are pleasantly surprised by how comfortable modern dentistry has become. Your comfort is our first priority.
How much does a consultation cost?
+
Your first consultation includes a free oral health assessment (valued at $80). We provide transparent pricing before beginning any treatment — no hidden fees, ever. We also offer flexible payment plans and work with most major insurance providers.
How quickly can I get an appointment?
+
We usually have same-week availability. For urgent dental issues, we offer emergency slots within 24–48 hours. Simply call us or use our booking form and we'll find the earliest convenient time for you.
Do you treat children and seniors?
+
Absolutely. We provide family dentistry for all ages — from a child's first dental visit to specialized senior care. Our team is trained to make every patient, regardless of age, feel safe and comfortable throughout their visit.
